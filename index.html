Quill analyzes your text into a data visualization, takes notes and allows users to share and impact those notes themselves. With our mobile app users take a photo of a body of text and Quill takes notes on and fully analyzes that text. Quill also has a web app so users can upload PDFs. Quill saves students hours on long reading and makes it easier to take notes.
