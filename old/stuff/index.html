What is The House

The House is the result of a question: "How can we make off campus life absolutely incredible?" We, along with our financial partners - billionare Las Vegas real estate developers - wanted to answer this question. How can we make living off campus totally awesome? This is our one goal. Because we are very well funded, we can afford to do it right. So we started by aquiring a beautiful property in downtown PA. Next, we plan to deck it out. Pool tables, XBox, a study, monitors, TVs, Ping Pong - you get the point.

At the end of the day however, what's most important, is you. The people living in The House. The House is designed to be a social destination. A place to hang out after a night at The Patio or Scotty's Bar. The House will hold one cohort of 6-8 people during the 2019/2020 year. Stanford University students who will be registered Sophomores, Juniors, Seniors, or Co-Terms during the 2019/2020 school year may apply in groups of 4, 6, or 8. One group (or two groups of 4) will be chosen to live in the house.

The application is open until June 1. Results come out in mid-July. If you are chosen to live in The House we will fully pay all on-campus housing cancellation fees. And not to worry, living off campus does not disqualify you from moving back onto campus in a later year. Finally the best part, it costs the exact same as living on campus. We match price down to the $$ (food is included btw).

Excited? So are we. Apply today.
